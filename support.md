## Common questions

### How do I add someone to my village?
Tap the **+** button in the Village tab and search for someone by name or phone number. They'll receive a request and need to accept before you're connected. Village connections are mutual — both people have to agree.

### How do I send a broadcast?
Tap the **compose** button in the Plans tab. Write what you're doing, pick who can see it (your inner, middle, or outer village), and post. Everyone in that audience will get a notification.

### How do I delete my account?
Open the You tab → tap **Delete Account**. Your profile, broadcasts, and all connections are removed immediately and permanently.

### How do I block someone?
Tap their profile → menu (•••) → **Block**. They can no longer contact you or see your content.

### How do I report someone?
Tap their profile or any broadcast → menu (•••) → **Report**. Tell us what's wrong and we'll review within 24 hours.

### I'm not getting notifications.
Check iOS Settings → Notifications → Village, and make sure notifications are allowed. If they are and you're still not receiving them, email us.

### How do I change my profile?
Open the You tab → tap your profile → edit your name, photo, or other details.
